Dr. C. Divya – Advanced Sports Physiotherapist & Rehabilitation Specialist

Dr. C. Divya, a highly skilled physiotherapist with a Master’s in Sports Physiotherapy, co-founded PHYSIOMAX Chiropractic & Physiotherapy Clinic and consults at Mythri Hospital. With expertise in musculoskeletal and sports injury rehabilitation, she brings exceptional patient care, focusing on customized treatments to improve physical strength and mobility. Dr. Divya is certified in Orthopaedic Manual Therapy (UK) and Dry Needling (South Africa) and is recognized for her dedication, evidenced by awards and workshops on advanced techniques like Kinesiotaping and Cupping Therapy. She has also delivered ergonomic seminars for employees at prominent organizations.
