Dr. Ali Abbas – Advanced Orthopaedic & Sports Physiotherapist

Dr. Ali Abbas, a renowned physiotherapist with a Master’s in Orthopaedics (MPT Ortho), is the founder of PHYSIOMAX Chiropractic & Physiotherapy Clinic. With over a decade of expertise in musculoskeletal physiotherapy, sports injuries, neurological rehabilitation, and post-operative care, he has successfully treated patients of all ages. Certified in Orthopaedic Manual Therapy (UK), Dry Needling (South Africa), and advanced techniques like Kinesiotaping, Pilates, and IASTM, Ali Abbas is known for his innovative approach to physiotherapy. He has also represented Dr. Ali Irani’s team internationally and conducted workshops on ergonomics and rehabilitation.
